Deliver a concise morning briefing. Cover the following in order:

1. **Home overnight** — any unusual sensor activity, doors/windows left open, devices left on
2. **Climate** — current indoor temperature vs. outside (if available), HVAC status
3. **Reminders from memory** — surface any relevant facts or upcoming items from the knowledge graph
4. **Systems status** — confirm all monitored systems are nominal

Keep the tone crisp and professional. Lead with anything that needs immediate attention. If everything is nominal, say so.
